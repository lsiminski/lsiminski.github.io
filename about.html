My name is Lauren Siminski. I am a designer based in Baltimore, currently attending the Maryland Instutute College of Art (MICA) for a BFA in Graphic Design.

I have had the privilage of working directly with the Woodberry Kitchen in-house designer in preparation for events such as the Star-Spangled Spectacular, the Baltimore Farmer's Market, & the recent Woodberry Pantry stand at John's Hopkins University.

I have done freelance work with clients such as Grilled Cheese & Co, Maryland Responds, Artful Libations, Birdcage Media, and other local businesses.

The majority of my inspiration comes from my sister and the rest of my goofy family. When I'm not working on a design project, I'm typically crocheting, discovering new games in the App Store to obsess over, or completely redecorating my room.
